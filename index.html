Lira

Lira, whose birth name is Lerato Moipone Molapo is a highly acclaimed South African singer. With multiple platinum-selling records and an impressive 12 South African Music Award (SAMA) wins under her belt, she has established herself as a leading Afro-soul vocalist. Lira's music is a unique blend of soul, funk, jazz, and traditional African elements.
Despite facing a major challenge after suffering a stroke in Germany that affected her ability to communicate, she courageously made a triumphant comeback performance. She continues to inspire with her passion for music and resiliency in the face of adversity.
